# Chemify — Interactive Chemistry Learning Platform

Chemify is a web-based platform designed to make learning chemistry engaging and practical.  
It offers tools such as a 3D Molecule Viewer, interactive Quizzes, a Unit Converter, and a detailed Periodic Table, all in one place.

---

## Overview

Chemify allows students, teachers, and science enthusiasts to explore chemical concepts in an interactive way.  
Whether it’s viewing molecular structures in 3D, converting scientific units, or testing your chemistry knowledge, Chemify simplifies complex topics through visualization and interactivity.

---

## Features

### 3D Molecule Viewer  
Visualize and interact with molecular structures in three dimensions.  
Users can rotate, zoom, and inspect molecules to better understand their atomic composition and geometry.

### Quiz Section  
An interactive quiz system that helps users test and improve their knowledge of chemical concepts and the periodic table.

### Unit Converter  
A comprehensive converter for temperature, pressure, volume, and other scientific units used in chemistry.

### Periodic Table  
An informative and easy-to-navigate periodic table that provides detailed information about each element including atomic number, atomic mass, and properties.
